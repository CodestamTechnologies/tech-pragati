Join us at Tech Pragati 2024

Tech Pragati 2024 emerges as a vibrant nexus where students, tech enthusiasts, industry leaders, and professionals converge to celebrate innovation, technology, and entrepreneurship. This groundbreaking event offers an immersive experience featuring engaging seminars, workshops, tech showcases, and invaluable networking opportunities. At the heart of Tech Pragati lies a dynamic array of challenges, including a stimulating hackathon, a fiercely competitive coding competition, thrilling robotics challenges, and an inspiring Ideathon. These hands-on competitions serve as the pinnacle of collaborative innovation, encouraging participants to push boundaries, solve real-world problems, and showcase their technological prowess.

Join us at Tech Pragati as we explore the latest technological advancements, connect with industry experts, and foster a collective passion for innovation.
